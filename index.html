Trend 1: The Mental Health Movement

Today, mental health is more openly discussed than ever before. Our generation has made therapy, emotional honesty, and self-awareness more acceptable. Yet, we must ask: is this awareness translating into real change?

Interventions

  • Social: Gen Z uses platforms like TikTok and Instagram to normalize therapy and vulnerability.
  • Political: More schools implement mental health programs and provide access to counselors.
  • Cultural: Celebrities like Billie Eilish and Sufjan Stevens speak openly about mental health.
